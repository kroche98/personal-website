Software Development

I’ve worked extensively with Python and JavaScript, used Docker and various SQL and NoSQL databases, and dabbled in C++, Java, Rust, LISP, and much more. My strategy combines an enthusiastic “jump in and start programming” attitude with a pragmatic attention to detail.
