Fifteen Puzzle

Rules

  1. Scramble the puzzle and then move the tiles, making use of the empty square, to return the tiles to their original configuration.
  2. The solved state has the tiles increasing in number from 1 to 15 with the empty square in the bottom right corner.
  3. To change the dimensions of the puzzle or the visual style of the puzzle, use the buttons below the display.
